# The locus

> **α** — What is a locus, and why is *everything* one?

This chapter is the foundational reading for the structural
model. It covers:

- The anatomy of a locus: `params`, `contract`, `bus`,
  `capacity`, lifecycle methods, modes, closures, member
  functions.
- The relationship between `locus` and `type` — types as
  proto-loci.
- Why every named structural thing in an Aperio program is a
  locus (the *one-tower* commitment) and what that buys you.
- A worked example growing in complexity through the chapter.
